Services

Delivery

Shirley Good Growing offers delivery from any of our connected greenhouses. We are able to do standard shipping, or rushed shipping with an extra fee. Our plants will be packed to ensure they arrive safely to your location. If your plants do not thrive within the first 2 weeks we will send a new plant, or refund the original price and shipping.

Landscaping

Shirley Good Growing contracts with local businesses to facilitate landscaping services. Shirley Good Growing sells materials to make your garden how you want, however, using our landscaping service, we will have a team help install anything purchased at any of our stores.
